### 5b. "Eleven Hidden Words in the Theme Entries"
The meta-instruction (clue 167) tells us:
1. There are **theme entries** in the crossword (the longer/special answers)
2. Hidden within those theme entries are **eleven words**
3. Those eleven words describe **what this puzzle commemorates**
4. The answer to 167-Across is whatever that commemorated thing is

**Technique for finding hidden words:** In crossword puzzles, "hidden words" means a word can be found spanning across consecutive letters within a longer entry. For example, if a theme entry is "ORCHESTRATE", the hidden word "CHEST" appears inside it (or-CHEST-rate).

### 5c. The Extraction Grid (Bottom-Right)
The separate grid in the bottom-right corner is the extraction mechanism. After solving the crossword:
1. Identify all circled letters in the main grid
2. Transfer them (likely in numerical order of their cell numbers) to the extraction grid
3. The extraction grid will spell out the final answer WORD for this sub-puzzle
4. This word is one of the 9 words in the meta-clue (expected lengths: 5, 9, 5, 7, 8, 4, 9, 6, 5)

The extraction grid appears to have space for roughly **40-50 letters** arranged in rows, which is much more than a single word. This could mean:
- The extraction grid spells out a phrase, not just a word
