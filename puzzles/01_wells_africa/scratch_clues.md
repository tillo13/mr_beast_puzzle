## Puzzle Mechanics

This is a **drop-quote style puzzle**. The grid contains both pre-printed letters and water-drop blanks. The 13 crossword-style clues below the grid produce answers whose letters fill into the water-drop blank cells. Once all blanks are filled, reading the grid left-to-right, top-to-bottom reveals a hidden message (likely a quote or instruction). The 5-letter answer word is then extracted from that message or from specific marked cells.

**Key mechanic:** In a standard drop quote, letters from above "drop" into blank cells in their respective columns. Here, the 13 clue answers likely provide the letter banks for each column -- their letters are distributed among the blank cells in the grid.

- **Reasoning:** The question mark signals wordplay. "A cold" = having a cold (illness). The noise of a cold = a sneeze = ACHOO. This is a well-established crossword clue pattern ("Sound heard during cold season" = ACHOO in NYT Mini Nov 2025).
- **Alternatives:** BRRR (4) -- noise from being cold temperature-wise; CHATTER (7) -- teeth chattering from cold; SNEEZE (6); SNIFFLE (7)

- **Letter count:** 5

- **Reasoning:** MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson) attended Greenville Christian Academy in Greenville, NC, graduating in 2016.
